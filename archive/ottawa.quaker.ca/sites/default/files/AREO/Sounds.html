"Quiet of Discernment"
Created: 12 May 2021
Posted: 15 May 2021
Creator: Jessica Jugenliemk
Length of Recording: 5m 21s
Click images below to Play or read Lyrics:

I had the pleasure this evening of facilitating a music therapy presentation/discussion/creative process with the Ottawa Monthly Meeting Friends, and crafting this song through the words that they offered on the topic of "discernment" (the process of seeking spiritual guidance, understanding, or clear perception). What a rich experience, and I hope you feel a piece of that in our song. <3
