This website is a place for Ottawa Quakers & their friends to share their creativity, inspiration and spiritual life.


*The fourth place (space) . . .

The first place is home, the second place is work, and the third place is environments where people gather to enjoy each other's company. (Arnault Morrison)

Now these places are merging resulting in the emergence of the fourth place. In these public environments one can hear one's own voice, work creatively and draw inspiration and security from those around them. In the fourth space people co-create, co-work, co-live and co-mingle.(Ray Oldenburg)
